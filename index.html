RTR Description

Our group name is Money Makers consists of Michael, Aaron, Kevin, Alan, and Franklin. Our app, Rags to Riches helps its users reduce monthly expenses by removing discretionary spending and helping them save money. Our app does this by intaking inputs such as monthly income, utility bills, and amount spent on entertainment. Then it displays how much they are spending in each category and how they can improve spending habits.
